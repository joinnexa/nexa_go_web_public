What is Nexa Go?

Nexa Go is the mobility layer of Nexa—a Morocco-first experience for rides, everyday delivery, and driver opportunities in growing cities.

Built for real city routines

Flows, routing, and onboarding are tailored to local habits. Users can request movement for people and packages; drivers and partners can access structured opportunities as the network expands.

Connected to the Nexa ecosystem

Nexa Pay anchors payments across Nexa (nexapay.ma), while the umbrella brand lives at joinnexa.ma. Nexa Go is the dedicated Nexa mobility surface.

Launch status

Nexa Go is under active development—cities, partner rules, fares, and product scope may evolve. Nothing on marketing pages constitutes legal or investment advice.
